Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to accessibility

BTR Directory is committed to making its website accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology.

We aim to provide a clear, usable, and inclusive experience for all users, including those who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or browser accessibility tools.

Accessibility standards

We strive to make BTR Directory accessible in line with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, targeting Level AA compliance wherever reasonably possible.

Accessibility is considered throughout the design and development process, including layout, colour contrast, typography, and interaction patterns.

What we're doing to improve accessibility

We are actively working to ensure that the site includes:

  • Clear and consistent page structure using semantic HTML
  • Keyboard-a`ccessible navigation and interactive elements
  • Readable text with sufficient colour contrast
  • Descriptive link text and headings for screen readers
  • Responsive layouts that work across deveices and screen sizes

Accessibility improvements are ongoing as the platform evolves.

Known limitations

While we aim to make the site as accessible as possible, some areas may not yet fully meet accessibility standards.

This may include:

  • Third-party content or external websites linked from BTR Directory
  • Legacy content added before accessibilty improvements were introduced.

We are commited to addressing accessibility issues as they are identified.

Third-party websites

BTR Directiry links to external operator websites for further information and enquiries.

We do not control the accessibility of third-party sites and cannot guarantee that they meet the same accessibility standards as BTR Directory.

Feedback and contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on BTR Directory, or if you have suggestions for improving accessibility, we welcome your feedback.

Please contact us via email, and we will do our best tp assist and address any issues.

Updates to this statement

This accessibility statement may be updated from time to time as the website evolves and accessibility improvements are made.

We encourage users to review this page periodically for the latest information.
