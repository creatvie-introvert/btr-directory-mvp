About BTR Directory

BTR Directory is an independent platform designed to help renters understand, compare, and explore Build-to-Rent developments across the UK — all in one place.

Our mission

Making Build-to-Rent easier to understand

Build-to-Rent is growing quickly, but information about developments is often scattered across multiple operator websites, each presented differently.

Our goal is to bring clarity to the rental search by presenting Build-to-Rent developments in a consistent, easy-to-compare format — so renters can research confidently before making enquiries.

BTR Directory is renter-first, independent, and focused on transparency rather than sales.

How it works

A simple way to browse and compare

BTR Directory brings together Build-to-Rent developments from across UK cities and displays them in a structured, comparable way.

You can use the site to:

  • Browse developments by city

  • View key facts, amenities, and pricing at a glance

  • Understand tenancy options and rental terms

  • Enquire directly about developments you're interested in

Everything is designed to help you make informed decisions before contacting an operator.

Data & accuracy

Where the information comes from

All development information on BTR Directory is sourced from publicly available operator websites and official marketing materials.

We aim to keep listings accurate and up to date, but details such as pricing, availability, and policies can change. For the most current information, renters should always confirm details directly with the development operator.

BTR Directory does not set prices or control availability.

Independence

An independent rental directory

BTR Directory is not a landlord, letting agent, or property manager.

We are not affiliated with any single operator, and listings are not endorsements. Our role is to organise and present information clearly, helping renters navigate the Build-to-Rent landscape more easily.

Enquiries

Getting in touch with a development

When you submit an enquiry through BTR Directory, your message is passed on to the relevant development or operator using the contact details available to us.

BTR Directory does not manage tenancies or handle bookings — all rental decisions and communications are handled by the operator.
