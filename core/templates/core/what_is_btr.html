What is BTR?

Build-to-Rent (BTR) is a modern way of renting where entire buildings or neighbourhoods are designed, owned, and professionally managed specifically for renters — not for sale.

The basics

What is it?

Build-to-Rent developments are purpose-built rental homes operated by professional landlords rather than individual property owners. Unlike traditional renting, where properties are often owned by private landlords, BTR developments are designed from the ground up with renters in mind.

Homes are typically modern, energy-efficient, and part of a larger managed community. Management, maintenance, and resident services are handled by an on-site or dedicated team, offering a more consistent and reliable renting experience.

Build-to-Rent has grown rapidly across UK cities, providing long-term rental options that prioritise quality, service, and stability.

How it compares

How is it different?

Compared to traditional private renting, Build-to-Rent offers a more structured and service-led approach.

  • Professional management rather than individual landlords

  • Purpose-built homes, not converted or resale properties

  • On-site amenities such as gyms, lounges, co-working spaces, and roof terraces

  • Clear tenancy terms with fewer unexpected changes

  • Long-term rental focus, not short-term investment

The result is a more predictable and transparent renting experience.

Who it suits

Who is it for?

Build-to-Rent appeals to a wide range of renters, including:

  • Professionals looking for flexible, high-quality city living

  • Families seeking stability without the commitment of buying

  • Remote and hybrid workers who value shared amenities and space

  • Renters relocating who want simplicity and professional support

BTR is particularly popular in major cities, where convenience, service, and location matter most.
